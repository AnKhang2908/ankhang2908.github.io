A visual resource for students to check rules, trigger words, and build perfect structures.

🔄 Present Simple ☕

Habits, Routines, and Permanent Facts

Use this for events that happen repeatedly, facts, truths, or regular daily schedules.

Structure:
Subject + Verb (s/es for He/She/It)
Example: "I cook dinner." / "She cooks dinner."
Signal Words (Click to inspect):
⚡ Present Continuous 🏃‍♂️

Actions Happening Right Now or Temporary Actions

Use this for actions occurring in this exact moment, short-term trends, or dynamic scenes right before your eyes.

Structure:
Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing
Example: "I am cooking." / "She is cooking."
Signal Words (Click to inspect):
usually Present Simple

Triggered actions happen habitually. 'Usually' suggests a 70-80% frequency pattern of behavior.
